Passive and private by design

The tag has no battery, no GPS, and no radio beyond a few centimeters. It can't track anyone and does nothing until a customer chooses to tap it. That's a cleaner privacy story than the email lists and app notifications they already tolerate, and a real selling point when you put it in plain words.
