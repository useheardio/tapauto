The plan

Three steps to customers who come back.

You shouldn't need a new system or a tech degree to keep the customers you've already earned. Here's the whole thing.

01

Peel and assign

Your tech peels a tag, taps it once to pair it with the car, and sticks it right where the paper reminder used to go. Seconds per car, nothing new to learn.

02

They hold their phone to the tag and their car comes up on the spot: history, what's due, and a one-tap way to book. No app, no QR code, no account.

03

They come back

Tap Auto reminds them right as they come due, with an offer timed to the moment, so the next visit is yours instead of the shop down the road.

Passive and private by design

The tag has no battery, no GPS, and no radio beyond a few centimeters. It can't track anyone, and it does nothing until a driver chooses to tap it. That's a cleaner privacy story than the email lists and apps they already put up with, and a real selling point once you say it out loud.
