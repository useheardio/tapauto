Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 21, 2026

This policy is a working draft written to reflect how Tap Auto handles data today. It is not legal advice. Have a qualified attorney review and tailor it for your jurisdictions before you rely on it in production.

Tap Auto provides a windshield tag and connected service that lets drivers tap to see their vehicle's service status and history, and lets the service shop that issued the tag keep accurate records and stay in touch. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Who is responsible for your data

The shop that gave you your tag is the business you have a relationship with. Tap Auto operates the technology on that shop's behalf as a service provider. When you tap your tag or use the app, you are interacting with both Tap Auto (the platform) and your shop (the business serving your car).

What we collect

  • Vehicle information: make, model, year, plate or VIN, oil type, and service interval.
  • Service records: the work performed, the mileage at each visit, dates, and the resulting service history.
  • Contact information you provide: your name, mobile number, and email address, only when you or your shop enter them.
  • Tap activity: when a tag is tapped, including a coarse, non-precise location and a security counter used to detect cloned or forged tags. We do not track your continuous location.
  • Account information: for shop staff, a login email and security credentials.

The tag itself is a simple pointer. It does not store your personal information, and an anonymous tap shows only a benign public view of the vehicle, never your name, contact details, or full history.

How we use it

  • To show your vehicle's status, health, and service history when you tap.
  • To let your shop maintain accurate records and serve your car.
  • To send you the service reminders and offers you have asked to receive.
  • To detect and prevent fraud, including cloned tags, and to keep the service secure.
  • To operate, support, and improve the product.

Text and email reminders (your consent)

We only send marketing or reminder texts and emails to people who have opted in.

  • Text messages: by opting in, you agree to receive automated reminder and offer texts from your shop at the number you provide. Consent is not a condition of any purchase. Message and data rates may apply. Message frequency varies. Reply STOP to cancel at any time, or HELP for help.
  • Email: by opting in, you agree to receive reminder and offer emails. Every email includes an unsubscribe link, and you can opt out at any time.

Transactional messages, such as a receipt for a service you just had, may be sent regardless of marketing consent because they are a direct part of the service you requested.

How to opt out

You are always in control. Reply STOP to any text to stop texts. Use the unsubscribe link in any email to stop emails. You can also ask your shop to turn reminders off, or contact us using the details below.

How we share data

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not run advertising against it. We share data only as needed to run the service:

  • Your shop: the business that issued your tag can see your records so it can serve your car.
  • Service providers that operate parts of the platform under contract, for example our database and hosting provider, our payment processor, and our email and text delivery providers. They may only use the data to provide their service to us.
  • Legal reasons: if required by law, or to protect rights, safety, and the integrity of the service.

How your data is protected

Access to shop and customer data is locked down so that each shop can see only its own customers, and a driver can see only their own vehicle. Anonymous taps never read personal data directly. Shop accounts support two-factor authentication. Tags can be provisioned with cryptographic signatures so a cloned tag is detected and rejected. We use encryption in transit and reputable infrastructure providers.

How long we keep data

We keep vehicle and service records for as long as the shop maintains its relationship with you and as needed for legitimate business and legal purposes. You can request deletion of your personal contact information as described below.

Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information, and to opt out of marketing. To exercise any of these, contact us or your shop. We will respond within the time required by applicable law.

Children

Tap Auto is not directed to children and is intended for vehicle owners and shop staff. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under the age required by law in their region.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the product evolves. When we make material changes, we will update the date at the top and, where appropriate, provide additional notice.

Contact

Questions about your data or this policy? Email hello@useheard.io, or contact the shop that issued your tag.
